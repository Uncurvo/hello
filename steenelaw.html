Steene Law's AI-Assisted 

Case Management


Redefining Legal Assistance 

We've developed a highly secure, privately hosted AI assistant that has revolutionized the way they manage cases and interact with their clients. 

  • The AI assistant we've crafted for Steene Law is not just a tool, it's a digital extension of their team. 
  • Capable of automatically scraping website URLs, and learning from diverse formats like PDFs, text files, and even video content, this assistant offers a new dimension of support. 
  • It enables Steene Law's team to converse with the AI about specific cases, laws, and unique client situations, thereby providing faster and more reliable information.

Key features​ 

  • Auto Knowledge Base: The assistant is trained on an extensive range of topics related to Steene Law, forming a comprehensive knowledge base that is continually updated and refined.
  • Auto Email Responder: For efficient client communication, the assistant manages email responses, ensuring timely and accurate information dissemination.
  • Case Compiler: It compiles and organizes case-related information, streamlining the process of case management and research.
  • Private Dashboard with Chat Transcripts & Exports: A secure and private dashboard allows for real-time monitoring of interactions, with the ability to export transcripts for record-keeping and analysis.

Prioritizing Security and Confidentiality:

Understanding the paramount importance of security and confidentiality in the legal domain, we ensured that the entire system is hosted privately. It features a private Large Language Model (LLM) managed by Uncurvo, ensuring that all data and interactions remain confidential and secure.

Transforming Legal Case Handling: 

Our AI solution has fundamentally transformed how Steene Law approaches new client cases. By grasping the complexities and nuances of law, the AI agent provides an invaluable resource for both the internal team and their clients. It not only enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of their legal services but also serves as a benchmark for technological innovation in the legal industry.
