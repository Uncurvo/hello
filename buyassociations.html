Elevating Client Engagement at 

BuyAssociation


Challenges

  • BuyAssociation, a company with global clients, faced the challenge of providing consistent support across different time zones with limited human resources. 
  • Understanding the critical role of relationships in their business, they turned to us to integrate AI into their workforce to enhance customer support and lead qualification.

Custom WhatsApp AI Agent: Your 24/7 Sales Partner


    To address this need, we developed a bespoke WhatsApp AI agent, effectively automating customer interactions and ensuring the team focuses only on qualified clients. Here’s how our solution redefined their sales and support processes:

  1. 24/7 Global Support: Our AI assistant offers round-the-clock support, addressing inquiries from different time zones, ensuring no client query goes unanswered.
  2. Efficient Lead Management: The WhatsApp agent plays a pivotal role in the sales process, engaging with new leads, conducting preliminary assessments, and gathering vital information.
  3. Seamless CRM Integration: Data collected by the AI is automatically logged and synced with BuyAssociation's CRM system, providing real-time lead tracking and facilitating efficient follow-up.
  4. Reduced Workload on Sales Team: By handling initial inquiries and lead qualification, our AI assistant significantly reduces the time the sales team spends on the phone, allowing them to concentrate on high-priority tasks and client relationships.



Features of the AI Solution:

  • AI Support Assistant: Mimicking the expertise and approach of their best sales member, the AI assistant efficiently handles a range of client interactions.
  • WhatsApp Sales Agent: Specifically designed for WhatsApp, this agent facilitates immediate and convenient communication with clients, enhancing engagement and response times.
  • Custom Knowledge Base Training: Continuously updated with BuyAssociation’s services, policies, and client care protocols, ensuring accurate and relevant responses.

Results:

The integration of our AI assistant into BuyAssociation’s operations has been transformative. 

  • It has streamlined their lead nurturing process, enhanced global client support, and optimized the efficiency of their sales team. 
  • By leveraging AI, BuyAssociation now enjoys a more efficient sales process, improved client satisfaction, and a significant reduction in workload for their human staff, all while maintaining the personal touch that is crucial in real estate dealings.
